Pasta Carbonara

Description

A classic Italian dish featuring pasta tossed in a creamy sauce with pancetta or bacon, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper. Quick and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 ounces pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan cheese; set aside.
  3. In a pan, cook pancetta or bacon until crispy. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add cooked pasta to the pan, tossing to combine with pancetta and garlic.
  5. Remove pan from heat and quickly stir in the egg and cheese mixture, ensuring the pasta is coated evenly.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your Pasta Carbonara!
